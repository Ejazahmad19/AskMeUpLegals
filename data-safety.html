🛡️ Data Safety

Your data security is our top priority. Learn about our comprehensive measures to keep your information safe and secure while using AskMeUp.

Security Standards: Enterprise-grade protection

🔐

Encryption Standards

All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Your information is protected with bank-level security standards throughout our entire infrastructure.

🏛️

Google Cloud Security

We leverage Google Cloud Platform and Firestore's enterprise security. This includes automatic backups, DDoS protection, and 24/7 monitoring by Google's security team.

🔑

Authentication Security

Google OAuth 2.0 authentication ensures secure sign-in without storing your passwords. We never see or store your Google credentials - authentication is handled directly by Google.

🚫

No Data Selling

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used solely to provide and improve the AskMeUp experience.

🔍

Minimal Data Collection

We only collect data essential for app functionality: display name, email, profile photo, and quiz performance. No sensitive personal information or device data is collected.

📱

Device Security

The app runs with minimal permissions and doesn't access sensitive device features like contacts, camera, or location.

🔄

Regular Security Updates

Our security measures are continuously updated to address new threats. We regularly patch and update our systems to maintain the highest security standards.

🛡️

Compliance & Standards

We follow GDPR principles for data protection and privacy. Our practices align with international data security standards and best practices for mobile applications.

⚠️

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident, we have procedures to immediately secure systems, assess impact, and notify affected users within 72 hours as required by law.

📞

Report Security Issues

If you discover any security vulnerabilities or have concerns about data safety, please contact us immediately through our support channels so we can address them promptly.
