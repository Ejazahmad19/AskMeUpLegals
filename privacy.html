🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how AskMeUp collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our quiz application.

Last updated: September 2025

📋

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google Authentication, we collect your display name, email address, and profile photo. We also track your quiz performance, points earned, and leaderboard rankings.

🎯

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to create your account, track quiz progress, calculate points, generate leaderboards, and provide personalized quiz experiences. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

🔐

Data Storage & Security

Your information is securely stored in Google Firestore with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

👁️

Public Information

Your display name, profile photo, and quiz points are visible to other users through the public leaderboard. Your email address remains private and is never displayed to other users.

🍪

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to improve app performance and user experience. These help us understand usage patterns and optimize our services for better functionality.

📊

Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as you use AskMeUp. Quiz history and points are preserved to maintain leaderboard integrity and provide continuous service.

✋

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Go through the app if you wish to exercise these rights or have questions about your data.

👶

Children's Privacy

AskMeUp is not intended for children under 7. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 7. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

🔄

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via app notifications or email. Your continued use indicates acceptance of updated policies.

📧

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the app's support section or check our FAQ for more information.
