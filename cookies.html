🍪 Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how AskMeUp uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and improve our services.

Last updated: September 2025

🍪

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites or use apps. They help remember your preferences and improve your experience by maintaining session information.

⚙️

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for basic app functionality, including user authentication, session management, and security features. They cannot be disabled as they're essential for the app to work properly.

📊

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with AskMeUp. This helps us identify popular features, improve performance, and enhance the overall user experience.

🎯

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and preferences, such as display options, quiz categories, and other personalization choices to provide a consistent experience across sessions.

🔐

Authentication Tokens

Secure tokens from Google Authentication are stored to maintain your logged-in state. These are encrypted and essential for keeping your account secure while using the app.

📱

Mobile App Storage

In the mobile app, we use local storage instead of traditional cookies. This includes cached quiz data, user preferences, and session information stored securely on your device.

🔄

Third-Party Services

Google services (Authentication, Firebase, Analytics) may set their own cookies. These are governed by Google's privacy policies and help provide secure, reliable services.

⏱️

Cookie Duration

Session cookies expire when you close the app, while persistent cookies may last up to one year. Authentication tokens are refreshed automatically for security purposes.

✋

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings or device preferences. However, disabling essential cookies may affect app functionality and your ability to stay logged in.

📝

Policy Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We'll notify users of significant changes through the app or this website.
